Chicken Alfredo Pizza

Description:

Enjoy this delicious Chicken Alfredo Pizza, bursting with creamy flavors and rich toppings. Tender chicken is spread over a crispy pizza crust, topped with a luscious Alfredo sauce and melted mozzarella cheese, creating an irresistible and satisfying culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust
  • 200g cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup Alfredo sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven according to the pizza crust instructions.
  2. Spread the Alfredo sauce evenly over the pizza crust.
  3. Arrange the sliced chicken evenly on top of the sauce.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the chicken.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.
Enjoy your delicious Chicken Alfredo Pizza!
