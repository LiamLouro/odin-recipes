Greek Pasta Salad

Description:

Enjoy this refreshing Greek pasta salad, bursting with Mediterranean flavors and vibrant colors. Al dente penne pasta is tossed with juicy cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, tangy feta cheese, briny black olives, and crunchy red onions, all dressed in a light and zesty vinaigrette. This delightful salad is perfect for a light lunch or a side dish, offering a healthy and satisfying culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • 250g penne pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and black olives.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top.
  5. Toss everything with your favorite Greek dressing and serve.
Enjoy your delicious Greek Pasta Salad!
