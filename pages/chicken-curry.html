Chicken Curry

Description:

Enjoy this delicious chicken curry with Indian rice, bursting with flavors and spices. Tender chicken is cooked in a creamy curry sauce, accompanied by soft and fluffy rice, creating an authentic and memorable culinary experience!

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cook the basmati rice according to the package instructions and set aside.
  2. Saut√© the chopped onion in a pan with oil until translucent.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in the curry powder and cook for one more minute.
  5. Add the coconut milk, bring to a boil, and simmer until the sauce thickens and the chicken is cooked through.
Enjoy your delicious Chicken Curry!
